## About Me

I’m Brian, a technology enthusiast with an A.A.S. in Computer Technology with an emphasis in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics from Tri-County Technical College. I have a strong foundation in IT systems, cybersecurity practices, and technical problem-solving, supported by hands-on experience across a wide range of tools, platforms, and programming environments.

I’m proficient with Microsoft Office applications (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Access, Outlook) and have practical experience working with MySQL, MariaDB, C, HTML, CSS, Python, and various scripting environments including Batch, Bash, PowerShell, and CMD. My technical interests extend into Linux administration—using distributions like Kali, Parrot, and Mint—as well as virtualization with VirtualBox and Raspberry Pi project development.

I’ve completed the ArcX Cyber Threat Intelligence certification, and I enjoy working with cybersecurity tools such as Wireshark and SDR#. My analytical mindset and curiosity drive me to continue learning, experimenting, and expanding my skill set in cyber and network security, digital forensics, threat intelligence, and system automation.

I am honored to be a member of both the National Society of Leadership and Success (NSLS) and Phi Theta Kappa (PTK), reflecting my dedication to academic excellence and professional growth.
